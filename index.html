Skyline Survivors

Defend the skyline, rescue humans from abduction, and survive increasingly brutal waves. Two modes available: classic wave progression and long-form survival.

Press R anytime on Game Over to restart.

From the Command Deck you can open the district map, pick a sector, and choose between wave (classic) or survival deployments. Rescue abducted humans mid-air for big bonuses and power-ups.
