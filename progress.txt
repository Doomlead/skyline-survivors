## What We Should Work On Next

The best next milestone is still **Challenge Mode Productization**, but now as a **finish-and-surface** pass (not greenfield).

### Why this should be next
- Existing mode-selection and run-summary plumbing reduces implementation risk.
- It resolves the most visible design-document compliance gap in a player-facing way.
- It unlocks replayability and cleaner balancing telemetry with minimal new combat content.
