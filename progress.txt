**Implementation Status: 78% Complete**  

### Key Themes Progress:

✅ **Fully Implemented:**
- Urban defense gameplay with district-based missions
- Ship/foot combat transitions via pilot ejection system
- Human rescue mechanics with landers and mutants

**Partially Aligned**

- Environment generators are broad, but weather/time-of-day reactivity is absent.

**Not Yet Aligned (Major Gaps)**

- Weather and district destruction-state reactivity layer.

- It gives challenge-mode and world-reactivity updates a stronger progression backbone for future telemetry and UX messaging.
