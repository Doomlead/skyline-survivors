Skyline Survivors: Implementation Progress

1. Core Concept

**Status: Fully Implemented**

All core themes are present in the codebase:
- Urban defense gameplay with district-based missions
- Ship/foot combat transitions via pilot ejection system
- Human rescue mechanics with landers and mutants
- Escalating difficulty through wave systems and boss encounters

---

2. High-Level Structure

## 2.1. System View Orbital Map

**Status: Partially Implemented**

✅ **Implemented:**
- 3D rotating globe with selectable districts
- District nodes with status indicators (threatened/cleared/destroyed)
- Special nodes: Weapons Satellite (Shop), relay stations, distress beacons
- District selection and launch flow
- Threat indicators with pulsing urgency rings
- District timers and state persistence

❌ **Missing:**
- Roaming enemy motherships that attack districts dynamically
- Enemy Base node (endgame assault location)
- Challenge Mission nodes
- Dynamic node threats beyond timers
- Global difficulty scaling triggered by destroyed districts

## 2.2. Side-Scrolling Combat View

**Status: Fully Implemented**

✅ **Implemented:**
- Horizontal wraparound combat arena
- Wave-based enemy spawning with district defense
- Human rescue mechanics (landers/mutants)
- Power-up collection system
- Mini-boss and boss encounters
- Radar HUD mini-map
- Procedurally generated parallax cityscape backgrounds

❌ **Missing:**
- Dynamic weather effects
- Day-night cycle affecting visibility
- Destroyable building interactions
- Mid-ground traffic/civilians layers

## 2.3. Meta Progression

**Status: Partially Implemented**

✅ **Implemented:**
- District clearing tracking
- Meta progression scaffolding (credits, unlocks, loadouts)
- Persistent storage and run history
- Failed defense consequences (district destruction states)

❌ **Missing:**
- Enemy base unlock system (50+ districts requirement)
- Progressive difficulty increase from failed defenses
- Campaign-wide progression gates
- Perfect run tracking and special rewards

4. Player Mechanics

## 4.1. Ship Forms & Movement

### 4.1.1. Normal Fighter Form

**Status: Fully Implemented**

✅ **Implemented:**
- 8-directional movement with inertia
- Forward blaster matching facing direction
- Smart Bomb (screen-clearing attack)
- Hyperspace (emergency teleport)

### 4.1.2. Guardian Form

**Status: Fully Implemented**

✅ **Implemented:**
- Toggle activation between forms
- Drastically slowed movement in guardian mode
- 360° mouse/controller aim
- Higher DPS special shot types

## 4.2. Ground Combat & Operative System

**Status: Partially Implemented**

✅ **Implemented:**
- Ship destruction triggers ejection sequence
- Pilot lands on foot near crash site
- Basic on-foot movement with gravity and jumping
- Re-entry to rebuilt ship after extraction

❌ **Missing:**

❌ **Missing:**

## 4.3. Power-Up & Weapon System

### 4.3.1. Power Sources

**Status: Fully Implemented**
